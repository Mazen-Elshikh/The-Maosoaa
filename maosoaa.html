HYDROPOWER

It is the energy of water that travels from heights to lower heights. It can be generated from reservoirs and rivers. Reservoir hydroelectric power plants rely on the water stored in the reservoir, while river hydroelectric power plants utilize energy from the available flow of the river. It is one of the oldest and largest renewable energy sources, and uses the natural flow of moving water to generate electricity. Hydropower currently accounts for 31.5% of all renewable electricity generation in the United States and about 6.3% of all electricity generation in the United States. learn more

Uses OF Renewable Energy

1- Uses of renewable energy in industry

The benefits that renewable energy provides to nature have been considered, so it is called clean energy or efficient energy, and the following are the most prominent use in the field of industry: Solar energy is used to generate heat, on which many industrial activities depend, such as paper mills. , factories specializing in mineral processing, and food factories. Solar panels are used to generate electricity for use in homes or factories. It is worth noting that the use of solar energy in generating electricity has witnessed a great development during the past decade. Wind energy has been used to generate electricity since ancient times, by converting wind energy into electricity. Wind energy has been used since ancient times to prepare spices, paints, cocoa, tobacco and dyes.

Uses of renewable energy in agriculture

Renewable energy is widely used in agriculture, and enables farmers to replace fossil fuel-based sources with renewable energy sources. The following are the highlights of renewable energy for the agricultural sector: Solar energy enables them to replace expensive diesel generators with less expensive and less harmful technologies. on the environment. Use of solar powered irrigation systems. Using wind energy to grind grain. The use of wind energy to pump water from wells.

Uses of renewable energy in daily life

There are many solutions around reducing the use of energy that pollutes the environment, and replacing it with renewable energy. It is necessary to know the uses of energy in our lives, and the following are some of them: Using electricity from solar energy to operate lighting instead of using electricity from traditional sources that pollute the environment. Providing homes with solar and wind energy to provide electricity and reduce the electricity bill. Using bio-ethanol as an alternative to gasoline, as it is obtained through the process of fermenting sugar. The use of cars that run on electricity as an alternative to cars that run on gasoline. The use of kinetic sidewalks, to benefit from the kinetic energy generated by them.
